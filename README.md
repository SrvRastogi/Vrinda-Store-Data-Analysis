# Vrinda-Store-Data-Analysis
This project focuses on analysing the sales data of Vrinda Store for the entire year of 2026. The objective is to analyse customer behaviour, identify purchasing trends, and deliver data-driven recommendations to help the business grow its revenue in the upcoming year. An Interactive Excel Dashboard was built to allow stakeholders to dynamically filter and analyse sales performance across multiple dimensions.
******************************************************************************************************************
Data Cleaning: Removed data anomalies, structural errors, and missing values.Standardised the gender column to fix inconsistent labels.Verified data integrity for orders, quantities, and amounts.

Data Processing: Extracted the billing month from dates using the TEXT formula. Grouped customer ages into customised demographic buckets using nested IF conditions.

Data Analysis & Pivot Tables: Built dynamic pivot tables to analyse relationships between sales variables.Formatted currency and revenue metrics into millions for better readability.

Data Visualisation & Dashboard Creation: Combined line and column charts on a dual-axis scale to compare sales against orders. Implemented pie charts to break down market share by gender and channels. Connected interactive slicers across all charts for synchronised filtering.
************************************************************************************
Insights														

Sales Peak: The highest sales and order volume occurred in the month of March.	

Customer Demographics: Women are the primary shoppers, contributing to the highest volume of sales.														

Order Status: The majority of orders have been successfully delivered.														

Top Performing States: The top 5 states contributing to total sales are Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, and Tamil Nadu.													

Customer Profile: The most active customer segment consists of adult women.														

Sales Channels: Amazon is the leading sales channel, accounting for approximately 35% of total sales, followed by Flipkart and Myntra.														
														
Final Conclusion to improve Vrinda store sales:														

Target women customers of age group (30-49 yrs) living in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra
